Adobo

Adobo the taste of every-Juan

This recipe is a symbol of Filipino cultural diversity from archipelagic native variants to foreign influences. Methods can become subjective to certain traditions, but it all resonates from its identical taste of culture.

Ingredients

  • Chicken/Pork
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorn
  • Salt
  • Bay leaves/Laurel
  • Water

Steps

  1. Crush the garlic.
  2. Boil along the meat all ingredients except laurel and vinegar, leave condiments for further tasting. It is better to add taste than insignificantly reduce later.
  3. Once it boils, add the vinegar and don't touch it for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add condiments if base on preference then add the laurel/bay seeds
  5. Pierce the meat using a fork to check if the meat is soft base on preference.
  6. Enjoy!
