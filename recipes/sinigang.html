Sinigang

Sinigang the fever killer

I woke up, then I have this bowl of sinigang that breaks through my fever and allow my taste buds to work, then my sweat flows out from my skin. By eating every ingredients I am just proud have parents with strong cooking traditional cooking style, here is their recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Calamansi
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Water Spinach
  • Okra
  • Long Beans
  • Daikon Radish
  • Green Chillies
  • Fish Sauce
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare the spices.
  2. Boil meat with onion, tomatoes, and garlic until tender.
  3. Add the souring agent and fish sauce.
  4. Add heartier vegetables (radish, long beans, okra) and simmer.
  5. Add Water spinach and green chilies last, cooking just until wilted. .
  6. Enjoy!
